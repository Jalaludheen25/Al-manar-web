Complete Piping Systems

Our pipes and fittings division provides comprehensive solutions for all your piping needs. From residential plumbing to industrial applications, we supply high-quality pipes, fittings, and accessories that ensure reliable and efficient fluid transport systems.

We stock a wide range of materials including PVC, CPVC, PPR, HDPE, and metal pipes with all necessary fittings and accessories. Our products meet international standards and are suitable for water supply, drainage, HVAC, and industrial applications.
